" We are transforming a broken expat student loan market "

I had the idea for Refiedu when my brother, after completing his education at Oxford, struggled to refinance his Indian student loan in the UK. He had a high paying finance job but financial institutions - both traditional and alternative - refused to refinance his loan.

We decided to dig deeper and found that the problem was widespread - International student loans that should have been re-rated continued to be mispriced. There had to be a better way and we decided to build it!

Shradha, Founder, RefiEDU
